About me

I am a 24 year old mechanical engineering student at the University of Iceland. I am currently on my third year for my bachelor degree. After I finished Upper Secondary School I decided to take a year off from school. I started to work at a Kindergarten to save up for a trip abroad. In January 2017, I went on a 4 month trip to South-East Asia, Indonesia, Australia, New Zealand and Fiji. I went along with three of my friends, and two of them are Aron and Alexander who are my current teammates in this course. My interests are Football, travelling and renewable energy. My favourite football club is Chelsea FC.
